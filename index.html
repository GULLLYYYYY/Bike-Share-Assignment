About Crusin'

Whether you are looking for a way to get to work, a family day out, or even just some exercise, Crusin' has the solution. With our state of the art bikes you can go anywhere in the city, in a sustainable fashion, from any one of our many locations in and around Bristol.

How Does it Work?

How to use
Firstly, you need to download the Crusin' app and make an account. Once you have registered, using the orange markers on our location map, find your nearest Crusin' station. Then simply scan the bike at your station, pay and ride!

Memberships and Pricings
